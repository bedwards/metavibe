Why Open Access?

These fields share research openly because:

  • Speed matters - Fast-moving fields where 18-month journal cycles are obsolete
  • Reproducibility - Code and data sharing enables verification
  • Public funding - Government-funded research mandates open access
  • Community norms - Key figures established open culture early

arXiv.org is the single most important resource with over 2 million papers across physics, math, CS, biology, and economics.
